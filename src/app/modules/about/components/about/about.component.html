About Us

My name is Mike Kelly, the inventor of the Mantis Mirror System. I am as passionate about bike riding now as I was when I got my first bike as a kid. Cycling keeps me healthy, helps clear my mind, let's me see so much of the great outdoors everytime I clip in. My bikes range from road; mountain; to commuter and I bike almost anywhere, as long as there’s no snow on the ground. I actually sold my car to allow (force) me to bike more often.

I created the Mantis Mirror System because I want my family, friends and every recreational cycling enthusiast, to feel safe by having a greater sense for their surroundings everytime they are on their bike.

With an extensive background in sales and marketing, in addition to prior experience bringing a new product from the “Napkin to the Shelf”, I bring a lot to the table.
