About Us

My name is Mike Kelly, the inventor of the Mantis Mirror System. I’ve been passionate about bike riding, ever since being a kid – now a senior citizen, that passion is just as strong. My bikes range from road; mountain; to commuter and I bike almost anywhere, as long as there’s no snow on the ground. I actually sold my car to allow (force) me to bike more often.

The problem, is that biking in general has become less safe, due to things like increased road traffic and of course, Distracted Driving! No matter how good a bike rider one is , when you don’t know what’s behind you, it can be an accident waiting to happen. Knowing what is approaching you is key to safer bike riding.

With an extensive background in sales and marketing, in addition to prior experience bringing a new product from the “Napkin to the Shelf”, I bring a lot to the table.
